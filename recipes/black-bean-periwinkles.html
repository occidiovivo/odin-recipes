Periwinkles in Black Bean Sauce

Index

A Favourite Snack from the East

Periwinkles are a unique snack, full of umami

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Periwinkles
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Thai Chilli Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Chopped Ginger
  • 2 tbsp Preserved Black Bean
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Dar Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Potato Starch
  • 1 Scallion

Steps

  1. Wash Periwinkles under water for 30 mins
  2. In a pestle, mash garlic, ginger thai chilli and black beans together
  3. Add periwinkles to a hot wok and add water. Cover and steam for 5 minutes
  4. Stir fry in mixutre from pestle and add vegetable oil, sugar and soy sauce. Cook for another minute
  5. Mix a tablespoon of water with potato starch to make a slurry. Add the slurry slowly to the wok while stiring constantly
  6. Remove periwinkles and serve in a bowl
  7. Chop scallion and place on top of the periwinkles before serving
