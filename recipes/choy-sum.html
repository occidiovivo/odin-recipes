Choy Sum in Oyster Sauce

Index

Amazing Simple Dish that Kids Will Love

Trouble having kids eat their vegetables? This quick 5 mintue dish will have them asking for more!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs of Choy Sum (Chinese Brocolli>
  • 2 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Water
  • 2 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tspb Minced Garlic
  • 1 pinch White Pepper
  • 1 Tbpn Potato Starch

Steps

  1. Cut off the ends of Choy Sum and clean thoroughly
  2. Add water to a wok and bring to boil. When boiling, add choy sum and cook for 1 minute
  3. Mix all other ingredients together to create the sauce
  4. Remove and drain choy sum
  5. On medium heat add oil to wok
  6. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds or until fragrant
  7. Add choy sum and stir fry on high heat
  8. Stir sauce together and slowly add to wok
  9. Stir fry until sauce thickens
  10. Serve and enjoy!
