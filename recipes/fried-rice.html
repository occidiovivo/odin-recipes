Fried Rice

Index

Create an Easy Quick Health Dish for the Family

Quick and delicous, this recipe that comes from Hong Kong, offers the family a satisfying meal that can be prepared in under 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Fried Rice
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Vegatable Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Rice Cooking Wine
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp White Peper
  • 1/4 Tsp Chicken Powder (optional)
  • 1/2 lbs meat of your choice
  • 1/4 Cup Carrots
  • 1/4 Cup Corn
  • 1/4 Cup Peas

Steps

  1. Mix all sauce ingredients together and stir well
  2. Pro-cook carrots corn and peas in a microwavable bowl. Add water and cook for 1 1/2 minutes (1000 watt microwave)
  3. Add oil to wok
  4. Under medium heat, add 3 eggs and stirfry until half cooked
  5. Turn heat to high and add rice. Stir fry the egg and rice together until the rice grains start seperating from each other
  6. Add sauce to rice and stir fry
  7. Remove rice and plaec aside
  8. Add oil and cook meat in wok under high heat
  9. Add vegatbles and rice to meat and stir fry until well mixed
  10. Remove and serve immediately
